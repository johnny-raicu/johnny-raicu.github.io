About Me


I am always searching for new challenges in my academics, music, and sports. And appreciate every obstacle thrown my way, as I know I will be stronger for it.

My name is Johnny Raicu and I live in Chicago’s suburbs. I am a passionate learner and innovative college bound senior eager to solve problems at the intersection of medicine and technology. I am a senior at Glenbrook South High School (GBS). As an aspiring physician, I am interested in STEM focused undergraduate program at an elite university at the intersection of medicine and computer science. My biomedical research internship at the University of Chicago in the summer of 2023 has fueled my desire to pursue more innovative research in biomedical engineering or neuroscience. I have taken the most rigorous coursework (10 Advanced Placement courses) in one of the best college-prep high schools. I am a section leader in the Glenbrook Symphonic Orchestra where I play the violin. I am a varsity tennis and soccer player. I lead the Medical Chapter Club at GBS. My other clubs include playing chess, piano, and ping pong. In the winter I love to ski, and in the summer I love to surf on Waikiki Beach and ride my jet ski on Lake Michigan.
